Advancements in information technology is leading the world altogether away from the traditional way of living to a new way of living that is heavily integrated with technology. The increased standard of living today has led to the pursuit of automated and convenient control systems. Automated systems will help people make their lives easier by taking care of the trivial tasks, leaving them to spend more time on the complicated tasks that require more resources. An example of this is that we were able to automate the lights of a room to switch off if there is no motion. This is the primary focus of our project. Systems like these will be extremely helpful for conserving power in big institutions. Most of the existing, well-established automation systems are based on wired communication.
